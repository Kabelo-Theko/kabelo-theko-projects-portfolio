serverless AIINVEST scoringTruth Guard

ProblemRequirements arrive as messy transcripts and email threads, and turning them into clean, testable stories is slow and easy to get wrong.
ApproachThe tool extracts a stakeholder map and INVEST user stories with Given/When/Then criteria; a deterministic scorer rates each story's Definition of Ready, and a Truth Guard flags any criterion that does not trace to a phrase in the transcript.
OutcomeStructured, testable stories in minutes, with hallucinated requirements made visible rather than slipping through.
